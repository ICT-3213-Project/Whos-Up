Description:
‘Who’s Up?!’ is a social app which will act as a virtual notice board for strangers to signal when they are free for an impromptu meet up or an activity.
People can post activities they want to host and others nearby can signal that they are “Up” for these activities.

Dependencies:
Internet connection
GPS in device
